Road Trip

a group of five Fung fellows including myself developed a smith group sensory therapy station to answer the need of senior residences who live in memory care communities. Our product got a lot of traction and is currently being evaluated before continuing to more advanced development stages.

We conducted a full product development cycle from user research to prototyping. our research showed that many of the residences who live in these communities miss the sense of driving but due to their health condition aren't allowed to drive. We decide to bring the intuitive and engage driving experiences into their homes using a RoadTrip station. The RoadTrip station gives residences the opportunity to drive with the safety of staying indoors in a controlled environment that is customized to their needs.
