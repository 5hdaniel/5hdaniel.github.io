Voice Controlled Car


During the secound semster of intro electrical engineering, I collaborated with Gautam Mittal and Matt Peng to develop a voice controlled car, we pre-selected four unique voice commands that mapped to four procedures: Go straight , turn left, turn right, go straight (long distance). To train the car's voice recognition algorithm we used PCA (principal component analysis).We used a training set of 30 two seconds recordings of each voice command

In the process of building the car we had to build a band pass filter that removed any sound outside of the human voice frequency using capacitors and resistors. We also implemented a closed loop control system that ensured the car can handel unexpected obstacles using speedometers of the left and right wheels
