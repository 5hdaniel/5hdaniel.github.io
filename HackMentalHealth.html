HINT OF FUN(G) - ShareEat

Hack Mental Health

UCSF 2019


I was invited to compete in the 2019 UCSF Hack Mental Health event as a part of my participation in the Fung Fellowship for Wellness and Technology. The hackaton aims to solve common public mental health challenges.

During our design processes we identified college students and older adults as the most impacted public populations. Our design proposed an integration solution to the loneliness epidemic by connecting these impacted population together using a Tinder like application that brings older adults and college students to share a meal and more. the older adults can share give the college students advice about life while the college students can help the older adults with technology.

My team HINT OF FUN(G) and I were the only team of students out of 60 teams of industry professionals that made it to the finals round and awarded with an Honorable mention.

We were also featured on one of the judges blog link.
